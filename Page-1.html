PRINCIPALS' MESSAGE

AMIT KUMAR
(PRINCIPAL)

With a determination to once again embark upon the mission of excellence and well prepared with the supreme art to inspire hope, ignite the imagination and to instil joy in creative expression and knowledge. We all, at IC KAKORE extend to you a warm welcome for the new session 2021-22.  We believe in the enunciation by Malcolm X that ‘education is a passport to the future, for tomorrow belongs to those who prepare for it today’. Therefore, for this session also, we have further established higher standards, greater heights and expectations for each and every child studying in the school.

INTERMEDIATE COLLEGE KAKORE strives hard to facilitate the holistic development of its students so that they canvigorously pay back to the society in all possible ways and can contribute to the development of the nationand humanity at large. We aim at developing in each child a scientific aptitude with a conscientious cognizance towards its ethical usage. Keeping in mind our core beliefs, i.e. our vision and mission to raisethem as soldiers, ready to face and clear all obstacles in life, we endeavour to provide the education that amalgamates mental and physical capabilities with discipline, governed by the timeless absolute values for‘education without values as useful as it is, seems rather to make man a more clear devil’.

The school provides a broad curriculum spectrum along with instructional expertise, emphasizing uponexploiting the potential of each student leading them to academic excellence of national benchmark. Butwe are well aware that all education is not confined to the four walls of the classrooms. A very well planned calendar for co curricular activities ensures the maximum participation of the students to display their talents in all the activities that facilitate the development of various domains of their mind and personality such as intellectual, emotional, social and moral development.

Life’s aspirations come in the guise of children. Everytime a child is born, it brings forth with him the hope that God is not yet disappointed with man.

FAMOUS POET

Rabindranath Tagore
