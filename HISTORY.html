Intermediate college kakore

History/Overview

Intermediate College Kakore was established in 1942 by Seth Vishambhar Dayal. It is Co-educational Govt. aided college located in Sikandrabad Block of Bulandshahr at Kakore Town.

Hindi is the medium of instruction in this college. Intermediate college takes pride of its mediate excellent teaching methodology.


 This college has 838 as student strength. This college is approachable by all weather road. In this college academic session starts in April. It has got 13 classrooms for instructional purposes. All the class rooms are in good condition. It has 02 other rooms for non teaching activities college has a separate office for principal and for clerk. College has electric connection. The source of drinking water is hand pumps and it is functional. There is a solar light on the college entrance gate which keeps the college lit when there is no light College has its own large playground.
